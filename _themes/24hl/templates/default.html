  • Hello, I'm Jarrod and I make things. I have a passion for design and a partcular interest in beautiful user interfaces.

  • 24hour lime is Digital Design. Web, mobile, devices, other things.

    Religion: The united church of UX focused design

  • I am a freelance designer and I work under the name 24hour lime. I got established mid-2014 and have been working with clients, trying to deliver the best possible designs ever since. Every day I get to learn new things while making people happy. What else could you ask for?

    To me, design is more than just communication or aesthetics - it is the examination and understanding of a problem, then coming up with a solution that fits within the given criteria and requirements. It provides functionality, has the user experience at the forefront, and also excites or delights. Design makes clients money, and changes how we live. That is a powerful thing.

    My focus is on UX-based design and my passion is UI. Give me the chance to design a new interface for a device or for the web and I'll happily run with it.

    If you are looking to hire a designer, get in touch. If it is an interesting or socially beneficial project, or you are a not-for-profit or charity, the quote may be very cheap (hint: free!).


  • If you like coffee and would like to catch up to chat about a project or need designer to help build your product, contact me below. I work out of an office in Brunswick East, Victoria, Australia.
